Our Story

Five Decades. One Promise.

In the 1970s, Laxman Prasad opened a small electrical shop in Lucknow's old city. The mission was simple: sell quality products at fair prices, and treat every customer like family.

Five decades later, that mission hasn't changed. We stock Meghdoot Supreme, Anchor, and Arkay Lite products. Honest advice, competitive prices, and service you can rely on, whether you're rewiring a whole house or replacing a single socket.
